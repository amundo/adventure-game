- GameManager.js - the entry point of the game, initializes everything else. - EnemyCharacter.js - represents an enemy, placed on the board by the GameManager. - GameItem.js - represents an item, placed on the board by the GameManager. - PlayerCharacter.js - represents the player, placed on the board at the beginning of the game. - GameUi.js - this lays out the game interface as instructed by GameManager. Generates terrain. - GameMap.js - the grid of cells. places items where instructed by GameUi. - GameCell.js - each cell in the grid. has an x, y property which places it in the grid. - GameControls.js - the controls that the user interacts with. also handles listening for key, etc events. - DataViewer.js - displays generic data.
