- GameManager.js - the entry point of the game, initializes everything else. - GameUi.js - this lays out the game interface as instructed by GameManager. - GameBoard.js - the grid of cells. - GameCell.js - each cell in the grid. - GameControls.js - the controls that the user interacts with. also handles listening for key, etc events. - EnemyCharacter.js - represents an enemy, placed on the board by the GameManager. - GameItem.js - represents an item, placed on the board by the GameManager. - PlayerCharacter.js - represents the player, placed on the board at the beginning of the game.
